Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How can I become a member?
Contact us via email or phone, and we'll guide you through our simple membership process. Membership is open to all Telugu families in Malaysia.
Are non-Telugu individuals welcome?
Absolutely! While we focus on Telugu culture, we welcome everyone interested in learning about and celebrating our heritage.
Do you offer Telugu language classes for children?
Yes! We conduct regular Telugu language classes for children and adults. Check our Events page for the current schedule.
How can I volunteer for events?
We're always looking for enthusiastic volunteers! Send us a message through the contact form or email us directly to learn about current opportunities.
What festivals do you celebrate?
We celebrate all major Telugu festivals including Sankranti, Ugadi, Bathukamma, Dussehra, Diwali, and more. Visit our Events page for the full calendar.
