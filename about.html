Our Story

Founded in 2010, the Klang Telugu Association has been the cornerstone of Telugu culture in Malaysia for over a decade. What started as a small gathering of Telugu families has blossomed into a thriving community of over 500 members.

We are dedicated to preserving our rich heritage while embracing the multicultural fabric of Malaysian society. Through cultural programs, educational initiatives, and community support, we create a home away from home for Telugu families.

What We Do

Our association organizes year-round cultural events, festival celebrations, and educational programs that keep our traditions alive. From teaching Telugu language to children, to hosting grand celebrations of Sankranti and Ugadi, we ensure our heritage thrives.

We also provide welfare support to community members, organize family gatherings, and create opportunities for networking and friendship among Telugu families in the Klang Valley.

To unite Telugu families in Malaysia, preserve our cultural heritage, promote Telugu language and traditions, and provide a supportive community network that fosters growth, celebration, and mutual assistance.
